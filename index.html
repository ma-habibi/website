JoyDom

AI-powered media generation

JoyDom is an AI-powered media generation service that leverages cutting-edge machine learning models to create videos and audio content automatically. It then publishes these creations to social media, offering a fully automated content pipeline. The name JoyDom is inspired by two iconic post-punk bands, Joy Division and Molchat Doma, reflecting the fusion of technology and artistic expression.
